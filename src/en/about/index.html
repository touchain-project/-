Company Profile

TouChain is a new-generation personal data management platform based on decentralized identity and a third-generation hardware wallet blockchain innovation enterprise. TouChain emphasizes that data should be the user’s personal assets. Its TouChain series of products creatively combine NAS storage and AI voice, and realize asymmetric encryption of data, digital asset management, and digital asset management through encryption chips embedded in the host. Privacy protection and other functions. Successfully opened up the connection between private cloud and blockchain, became a window for users to touch the world of blockchain, and provided a good foundation for decentralized product services and applications.

Touchain hopes to use blockchain technology to realize the safe storage, management and circulation of data. And through DID and its own Touchain public chain, while protecting user privacy, it manages user data through smart hardware Alpha, so that the data generates its due value for users.
